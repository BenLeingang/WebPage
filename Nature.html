Nature and Activities!

Castle Rock offers some of the best nature and sights to see.
Activities included:

  1. Hiking
  2. Biking
  3. Ski and Snowboarding
  4. Fishing

The Rock Park

The Rock is a beautiful hike that looks over all of Castle Rock. It is not very hard, so it's more fun. Highly recommend this to anyone visiting.

The MAC

Also known as The Miller Activity Complex, is a great place very anyone to do anything active. They include a jungle gym, pool with a slide, sports field, the mini incline(200 steps), trampoline park, and a zipline course. They also have a amphitheater and tables for public use and mini festivals.

The Outlets In Castle Rock

The Outlets are crammed full of local and commercial businesses. You can spend a full day of shopping here and then settle down at the lovely food court inside.
