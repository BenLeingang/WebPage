Food In Castle Rock

Castle Rock has found its niche with more than 100 dining venues to tempt you. Local family owned restaurants in such unique settings as a historic Western saloon, a stone church or a trendy micro brewery will serve up memorable meals that reflect a wide range of cuisine.

Castle Cafe

Castle Cafe is a great cafe with the classic extreme variation in food and drink. The interior resembles a classic western saloon. A great choice after church on Sunday!
4 Stars

The Office

The Office Bar provides you with a wide variety of food, such as, seafood, burgers, pizza, wings and many more. This is the place to go on Friday and Saturday to meet some of the best people in Castle Rock.
4.3 Stars

B&B Cafe

B&B Cafe offers great breakfast and with its great old interior you'll never get bored.
